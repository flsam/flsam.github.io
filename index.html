Welcome to FLSAM

Who We Are

FLSAM is a group of math competitors across the state of Florida that come together to focus on higher-level mathematics and train for contests. If you're looking for something a little bit more than Mu Alpha Theta, then look no further.

What We Do

FLSAM currently sends representative teams to national tournaments including ARML, CMIMC, PUMaC, HMMT. Throughout the school year we hold a series of meetings across the state dedicated to learning and practicing the mathematics required of us to do well.

A Little History

Founded in 2006 by a group of dedicated mathematics students, the Florida Student Association of Mathematics (FLSAM) serves to organize teams that will represent the state of Florida in various mathematics competitions at the state, regional, and national levels.

We select students from across the state to join teams and to compete in prestigious out-of-state mathematical competitions such as the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament, the Princeton University Math Competition, and the American Regions Math League. We also have written and hosted our own contests in the past including the Florida Math Challenge (results) and FLSAM Winter Contest. By doing so, FLSAM strives to reach its goal of building a culture of opportunity for mathematically talented students and expanding the mathematical involvement of students beyond that of what is typically taught in schools.

FLSAM is divided into regions equivalent to those of the Florida Association of Mu Alpha Theta to maximize the competitive spirit of its members. We encourage all those who are enthusiastic and passionate about mathematics to join!
