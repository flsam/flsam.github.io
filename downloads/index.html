Downloads

Here are some of the tests from tryouts (ARML, PUMaC, HMMT) and events (Spooky Halloween contest, FLOMO) that FLSAM has organized in the past few years. The Spooky Halloween contest is a fun contest around mid-AIME level. FLOMO is a 50 short-answer team contest from Mathcounts level to mid-AIME. To get each year’s test, simply find the year in the dropdown menu, click on it, and click on the specific test within the result box.
